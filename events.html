Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid ul-Fitr, or the "Festival of Breaking the Fast," is celebrated by Muslims worldwide to mark the end of Ramadan. The date varies each year, depending on the sighting of the crescent moon, and signifies the start of the Islamic month of Shawwal. It is a time for gratitude, charity, and joyous gatherings with family and friends.

Diwali

Diwali, or the "Festival of Lights," is a major Hindu celebration that signifies the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Celebrated annually in October or November, the date varies based on the lunar calendar. The festival involves lighting oil lamps (diyas), decorating homes, bursting fireworks, and sharing sweets with family and friends.
